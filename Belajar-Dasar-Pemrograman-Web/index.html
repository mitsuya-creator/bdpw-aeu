As Everything Unfolds

As Everything Unfolds

is a British metal band from High Wycombe, Buckinghamshire, England.

History

The band formed in 2013 as an acoustic pairing of Alex Paton and current guitarist, Adam Kerr.[2] Their second EP, Closure, was released in 2018 to positive reviews.[3][4] In September 2020, the band signed with Long Branch Records, an imprint of SPV Schallplatten.[5] They released their first full-length album, Within Each Lies The Other, on 26 March 2021.[6] On 13 May 2022, Owen Hill left the band on good terms to pursue new goals.[7] In January 2023, the band announced their second album, Ultraviolet, would be released on 21 April.[8]

Musical Style

The group's sound incorporates elements of alternative rock, post-hardcore, and progressive metal, while lead singer Charlie Rolfe's vocal style has been described as a mixture of Hayley Williams, Amy Lee and Becca Macintyre.[9][10][11]
